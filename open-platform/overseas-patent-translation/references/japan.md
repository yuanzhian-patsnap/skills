# Japan Patent Translation Reference

## Target Language

Use Japanese. Format for Japan patent application drafting.

## Drafting Focus

- Use Japanese patent style rather than Chinese-literal wording.
- Keep "部", "手段", "装置", "工程", and "ステップ" consistent with the technical field and claim category.
- Make claim sentences clear and grammatically complete.
- Preserve priority support and avoid adding embodiments not present in the Chinese source.
- Express technical effects concretely and moderately.

## Recommended Section Order

```text
発明の名称
技術分野
背景技術
発明の概要
図面の簡単な説明
発明を実施するための形態
特許請求の範囲
要約書
図面
配列表（該当する場合）
```

## Claim Style Notes

- Use "備える" for open-ended "包括/包含" when appropriate.
- Keep antecedent clarity for components and relationships.
- Translate "用于..." as a functional relation only when needed; prefer concrete structure or processing where supported.
- Avoid overly long claim clauses that obscure modifier relationships.
- Maintain consistency between claim terms and embodiment terms.

## QA Focus

- Check whether Chinese-style parallel phrases became ambiguous in Japanese.
- Check that reference numerals and drawing labels match Japanese terms.
- Check that "好ましくは" and optional embodiments remain optional.
